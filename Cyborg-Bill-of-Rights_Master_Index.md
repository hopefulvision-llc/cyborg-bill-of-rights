🧭 What Is the Cyborg Bill of Rights?

The Cyborg Bill of Rights is not science fiction. It is not a marketing manifesto. It is not a transhumanist fantasy.

It is a preemptive human-rights charter for the coming reality of hybrid embodiment.

It represents:

The protection of bodily autonomy in an age of implants, prosthetics, and neural interfaces

The protection of mental autonomy in an age of predictive and assistive AI

The refusal to let enhanced humans become products, platforms, or property


Core principle:

> Augmentation does not reduce a person’s humanity. It increases our responsibility to protect it.

🧍 Core Rights Domains

🧠 Mental Sovereignty

Your thoughts are private

Your cognition cannot be remotely modified without consent

No forced updates to your mind


🫀 Bodily Sovereignty

Implants and prosthetics are part of your body

They cannot be disabled, repossessed, or altered without consent


🧾 Consent & Transparency

You must know what your systems do

You must be able to refuse updates

You must be able to exit


⚖️ Legal Personhood Continuity

You do not become a different legal class because you are augmented
